Who are we?

LoveFood® is on a mission to redefine the waste management industry, by educating private and business cutsomers, with data collected, and presented by the worlds most renowned food waste researchers.

We believe a data-driven approach is the key to kickstarting a smart waste management revolution.

With almost 90% of the world’s food waste being handled incorrectly, LoveFood® was born out of a desire to address the inefficiency and lack of sustainability in traditional waste management.
